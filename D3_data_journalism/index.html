Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

This chart presents six different parameters of the population accross all states of USA: poverty level (%), median age, median income, percent of population, who lacks healthcare, smokes or suffer from obesity.

The idea of this research was to understand existing correlation between such factors as poverty, age and income from one side, and lack of healthcare, smoking and obesity from the other.

As far as we can see from the chart above there is a clear correlation between: - size of income and lack of healthcare; - size of income and obesity; - poverty and obesity; - age and smoking.
